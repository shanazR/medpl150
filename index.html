Welcome, my name is Shanaz. My work mostly demonstartes simplisitic designs and techniques, with a hint of color and vibrancy. I would like for my work to be seen and understood as concepts familair to mostly all individuals, stretching from the generation before us to the the current one today. Traditional and nostalgiac imagery is what I want to express through my desings and pieces of work. It could range from lifestyle, culture, and other relatable concepts. I want to utilize materials and styles that are easy for both me and the audience. Seeing these pieces of work should be a breath of fresh air, nothing intense or complex.
